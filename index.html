Counting Robots

Instructions

The robots are trying to take over the world and the only way they can be stopped is to beat them at their own game. You will be given a random number as your Targert Score to start the game. Each robot head is programmed with a specific number. You must click whichever robot head to achieve the generated Target Score. Learn which head is what number and reach the Target Score. If you over estimate the Target Score the robots will win, if you achieve the Target Score, you win.
